## ü§ñ AI Tools - Step-by-Step Guide

### **Tool 1: Contract Analyzer**

**When to Use:**
- Reviewing client contracts
- Vendor agreements
- Employment contracts
- Service agreements

**How to Use:**
1. Go to **Dashboard ‚Üí AI Tools ‚Üí Contract Analyzer**
2. Paste contract text or clauses into the input box
3. Click "Analyze"
4. Review the risk assessment
5. Export results if needed

**Example Input:**
```
Section 12: Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement with or without cause 
upon 30 days written notice. Upon termination, all obligations 
shall immediately cease.
```

**What You'll Get:**
- Risk level indicators (üî¥ High, üü° Medium, üü¢ Low)
- Specific clause issues
- Suggested improvements
- Missing provisions alert

**üí° Pro Tip:** Always review AI suggestions with a senior attorney before finalizing.

---

### **Tool 2: Document Generator**

**When to Use:**
- Creating NDAs
- Drafting retainer agreements
- Standard form letters
- Client contracts

**How to Use:**
1. Select **Document Generator** from AI Tools
2. Describe what you need:
   - Type of document
   - Parties involved
   - Key terms
   - Duration
3. Click "Generate"
4. Review and customize the draft
5. Export to Word/PDF

**Example Request:**
```
Create a mutual non-disclosure agreement between:
- Party A: TechCorp Inc. (software company)
- Party B: Legal Services LLC (law firm)
- Duration: 2 years
- Purpose: Discussing potential partnership
```

**What You'll Get:**
- Complete document draft
- Standard legal provisions
- Placeholder fields for customization
- Professional formatting

---

### **Tool 3: Case Law Researcher**

**When to Use:**
- Preparing motions
- Writing briefs
- Client consultations
- Legal memos

**How to Use:**
1. Open **Case Law Researcher**
2. Describe your legal issue
3. Include jurisdiction if known
4. Review suggested cases
5. Copy search terms to Westlaw/Lexis

**Example Query:**
```
Employment discrimination case in California.
Female employee passed over for promotion after pregnancy.
Need cases about pregnancy discrimination and adverse employment actions.
```

**What You'll Get:**
- Relevant case suggestions
- Key search terms
- Jurisdiction guidance
- Citation format

**üí° Pro Tip:** Use the suggested search terms in your legal research database for comprehensive results.

---

### **Tool 4: Legal Writing Assistant**

**When to Use:**
- Polishing briefs
- Improving client letters
- Editing motions
- Professional communications

**How to Use:**
1. Paste your draft text
2. Specify what you want improved:
   - "Make more persuasive"
   - "Improve clarity"
   - "Strengthen legal arguments"
   - "Fix grammar and style"
3. Review AI suggestions
4. Apply changes selectively

**Example Input:**
```
The defendant did bad things and caused harm to my client. 
They should be held responsible for their actions and pay damages.
```

**What You'll Get:**
```
Defendant's conduct constitutes material breach of contractual 
obligations, directly causing foreseeable damages to Plaintiff. 
Pursuant to [legal standard], Defendant is liable for [specific damages].
```

---

### **Tool 5: Deposition Summarizer**

**When to Use:**

**How to Use:**
1. Copy deposition transcript
2. Paste into Deposition Summarizer

**What You'll Get:**
- Timeline of events
- Key admissions highlighted

- Follow-up question suggestions

### **Tool 6: Brief Analyzer**

**When to Use:**
- Final brief review
- Before filing motions
- Quality assurance

**How to Use:**
1. Upload or paste brief text
2. Select analysis type:
   - Complete review

3. Review critique
4. Make improvements
5. Re-analyze if needed

**üí° Tips for Good Questions:**

## üõ°Ô∏è Understanding Content Moderation

### **Why Content is Moderated**

**To Keep Community Professional:**
- Removes spam and promotional content
- Filters toxic or abusive language
- Ensures legal-domain focus
- Protects user experience

### **What Gets Flagged**

**Automatically Rejected:**
- ‚ùå Hate speech or harassment
- ‚ùå Profanity or abusive language
- ‚ùå Spam or advertising
- ‚ùå Off-topic discussions
- ‚ùå Non-legal questions

**Sent to Review Queue:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Potentially offensive language
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Borderline spam
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Unclear if legal-related

### **If Your Content is Flagged**

**1. Check Your Email**
- You'll receive notification
- Explains why content was flagged
- Provides review link

**2. Review Decision**
- Log in to see full details
- Understand the specific issue
- Learn for future posts

**3. Appeal (If You Disagree)**
- Click "Appeal Decision"
- Provide reason for appeal
- Admin will review within 24 hours
- Decision is final

### **Tips to Avoid Flagging**

**‚úÖ Do:**
- Use professional language
- Stay on legal topics
- Provide constructive feedback
- Ask clear, relevant questions
- Cite sources when possible

**‚ùå Don't:**
- Use profanity (even mild)
- Post promotional content
- Attack other users
- Ask non-legal questions
- Post duplicate content
