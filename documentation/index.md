## üåü Key Features

The Advyon Legal Platform is built to modernize legal workflows:

- **AI-Powered Insights**: Leveraging Google Generative AI for document analysis and case summaries.
- **Smart Document Management**: OCR, auto-tagging, and secure storage with Cloudinary.
- **Case Management**: Complete lifecycle management for legal cases and matters.
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Built-in messaging and notification systems.
- **Gamification**: Activity tracking and rewards for user engagement.
